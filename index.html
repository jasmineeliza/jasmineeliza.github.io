ABOUT SOMA

La Vie en Shih Tzu.

Hello, and Welcome to Soma Shih Tzu. We practice small-scale, ethical breeding to ensure the health and longevity of all puppies born to Soma. We work closely with the San Diego Bay Animal Hospital, to make sure our pups are up to date on shots and worm medication.

In addition to the well-being of our own pups, we are proud supporters of the Shih Tzu Rescue INC., whom of which do wonderful work. We donate 5% of each puppy sale to the 501(c) charity to help disadvantaged Shih Tzus' find their fur-ever homes. We love the Tibetan Shih Tzu, and want to preserve the wonderfully joyus toy breed for years to come.

I often receive questions about Shih Tzu puppy growth and full-grown weight projections. I have created a chart below to explain the growth rate and given some equations you can do to project a full-grown weight.

Puppy Growth Chart

PUPPY AGE WEIGHT (LBS) PROGRESS IN TERMS OF GROWTH EQUATION EXAMPLES (FOR ESTIMATED ADULT WEIGHT)
4 WEEKS .75 to 1.5 A Shih Tzu is at about 10% of adult weight. 1.25 lbs. X 10 = estimated adult weight of 12.5 lbs.
8 WEEKS 1.75 to 2.25 The Shih Tzu puppy is now at roughly 20% of his adult size. 2.25 lbs. X 5 = estimated weight of 11.25 lbs.
3 MONS. 3.5 to 4.5 The puppy is now just about 1/3 of his/her adult weight. 4 lbs. X 3 = estimated weight of 12 lbs.
